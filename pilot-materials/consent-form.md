## What this study is

You are being invited to participate in a small pilot study for a new instrument that measures how people make small ethical choices in everyday situations. The instrument is delivered as a daily-session app: about five minutes per day for four weeks.

The study is small (n = 10) and exploratory. The instrument has not yet been validated — that is, we do not yet know whether the scores it produces accurately reflect anything meaningful about how you actually live. **You are not being measured; you are helping us figure out whether the instrument works.**

If that distinction is important to you, this is the right moment to step away. We will not contact you again, and no record will be kept of your having considered participation.

---

## What participation involves

If you choose to participate:

1. **Daily sessions** (4 weeks). About 5–10 minutes per day. You will make small choices in described scenarios, sort and compare values, and occasionally answer short open-ended questions. There is no right or wrong answer to any of it; we are interested in your patterns, not in scoring you against a benchmark.

2. **Weekly check-in interviews** (30 minutes each, 4 total). A researcher will ask about your experience of the app — what worked, what didn't, what surprised you. These are about the instrument, not about you. You can decline any specific question without explanation.

3. **Exit interview** (45 minutes, once at the end of the study). A more structured conversation about your experience, scenarios that stood out, and any concerns you have about how the data was collected.

4. **Optional informant report** (week 4, with separate consent from informants). Two people who know you well (one from your personal life, one from a professional context) will be invited to fill out a short questionnaire about you. This is opt-in; you can complete the rest of the study without it.

**Total time commitment:** approximately 5 hours over 4 weeks.

---

## Risks

This is what we know about, and what we want you to know:

- **The instrument is unvalidated.** Scores it produces about your patterns may not reflect anything real. We will not show you any scores during the pilot, and we will not publish results from this study.

- **Self-reflection may be uncomfortable.** Some scenarios touch on situations where users describe themselves choosing to fall short of who they want to be. This is part of the design. We do not think it is harmful at typical levels, but if you find yourself becoming preoccupied with self-monitoring or anxious about your moral self-view, we encourage you to stop the study and reach out to the researcher.

- **Some scenarios involve sensitive topics**: family conflict, workplace ethics, lying in close relationships, financial pressure, group exclusion dynamics, and longer-term commitment dilemmas. None involve graphic content; all are at the level of recognizable everyday situations. You can skip any item without penalty.

- **Time commitment.** Four weeks of daily sessions is real. The full $50 compensation is contingent on completing at least 14 sessions and the exit interview; smaller compensation tiers apply for partial completion.

- **No therapeutic benefit.** This is a research instrument, not therapy or coaching. If you are in crisis or have ongoing mental health concerns, please prioritize the care you have access to over participating here.

Crisis resources: [988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline, your country's equivalent, links to be inserted per locale].

## What we do with your data

- **Local-first by default.** Your session responses are stored on your device. Only aggregated, opt-in data is transmitted to our research server for the pilot.

- **End-to-end encrypted when synced.** If you opt into the cloud sync option, your data is encrypted with a key only you control; we cannot read individual responses.

- **No training on your data.** Your responses will not be used to train any AI model, public or private.

- **Discarded after analysis.** Pilot data is discarded within 90 days of pilot completion. We retain only the aggregated quantitative metrics (response times, completion rates, scenario-flag counts) and our anonymized interview transcripts. Free-text responses are deleted from server storage.

- **You can withdraw at any time.** Email us at the contact above and your data is deleted, including the aggregated metrics. No questions, no follow-up.

- **No sale, no enterprise, no external sharing.** We do not sell or share data with employers, partners, or third parties. We do not maintain any database that any external party could query.

## Right to withdraw

You can withdraw at any point, for any reason, without explanation. Withdrawal can be:

- **Quiet withdrawal**: stop opening the app. Your participation lapses; you get the prorated compensation; no follow-up.
- **Active withdrawal**: email us and we delete your data. You receive prorated compensation up to your withdrawal date.

There is no penalty for withdrawal beyond the prorated compensation.
